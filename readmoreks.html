web-based application

Introduction

Generally there a traditional system in our universities program for every type of work from assignments to submissions,mid-semester exams to regular knowledge test, sharing of knowledge and time on specific task to a little audience about 50-60 students are usually done manually which is tedious, boring and slow process for a student as well as teacher to learn new things around them .

KnowledgeShala is a web-based application that offers the social media platform by which students of college/university can interact with the instructor by sharing thoughts, knowledge and time.

Existing System

Facebook is a social networking website where users can post comments, share photographs and post links to news or other interesting content on the web, chat live, and watch short-form video. You can even order food on Facebook if that's what you want to do. It is a single platform for all users i.e common for all people platform which doesn't provide a private networking site for a particular public. It provides CREATE A COMMUNITY PAGE service, which isn't enough to give full exposure and specific universities network services and feel.

Need for the new System

As an IT discipline student, the main objective is to be updated to each and every new technology that has been unrevealed in this fast-growing world and the social media network is the one that caught almost every techie and non-techie eye people to create a huge impact on their lives. After facebook, the impact of social media changed like revolutionary change in the world which was a common platform for all types of users. Like Linkedin is for people who are keenly interested in job- relevant things likewise there's a need for private universities network to create ease in life of a student and teacher.

Objective for new System

A student will be able to send requests and share their thoughts and ideas relevant to their branch on the timeline. An Instructor can get followers and star badges based on the quality of their posts. The student will get the posts on their newsfeed which is only related to their skills and field. Instructor ID will be verified by the college and student ID will be verified by their own department instructor provided by their college.
